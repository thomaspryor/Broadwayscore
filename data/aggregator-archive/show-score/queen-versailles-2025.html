\nAbout the Show\n

\n
\n
\n

A lavish musical about Jackie Siegel’s rise, wealth, and struggles amid the 2008 recession.

\n
\n

From computer engineer to beauty queen to billionaire socialite, Jackie Siegel sees herself as the ultimate embodiment of the American Dream. As the wife of David Siegel, known as “The Timeshare King,” and the mother of their eight children, she embarks on an extravagant endeavor: constructing the largest private home in America—a $100 million mansion in Orlando, Florida, inspired by the grandeur of the Palace of Versailles. However, as the 2008 financial crisis approaches, the Siegels’ lavish aspirations begin to collapse along with their opulent lifestyle.

Based on the astonishing true story captured in the acclaimed documentary by award-winning filmmaker Lauren Greenfield, The Queen of Versailles is now a new musical. Featuring a score by Academy Award®–winning songwriter Stephen Schwartz and starring Tony® and Emmy Award® winner Kristin Chenoweth as Jackie Siegel. This dazzling musical delves into the price of wealth, ambition, and the pursuit of an extravagant dream.
