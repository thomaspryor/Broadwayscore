About the Show

Billy Crystal co-writes and stars in this brand-new musical comedy premiering on Broadway.  

In this musical based on Billy Crystal's 1992 movie, Buddy Young Jr. is an outrageous and outspoken comedian who found fame, if not fortune, in the early days of television. Now, about four decades later, he seeks one more shot at the spotlight and, while heâ€™s at it, one last shot at fixing the family he fractured along the way. Featuring music by 3x Tony winner Jason Robert Brown, lyrics by Tony nominee Amanda Green, and direction by Tony winner John Rando.

In addition to Crystal, the cast also includes Randy Graff ('City of Angels'), David Paymer ('City Slickers'), and Shoshana Bean ('Wicked').

The new musical was nominated for 5 Tony Awards, including Best Musical, Best Book of a Musical, and Best Original Score. Crystal and Bean were also nominated for their performances.
