About the Show

Wildly relevant & bitingly funny, Eureka Day tackles liberal vaccine policy amidst a mumps outbreak

"Eureka Day" by Jonathan Spector is a play that seamlessly blends relevance with humor, offering audiences a compelling exploration of contemporary issues. 

As cases of the mumps continue to rise within the school, the board members find themselves grappling with a dilemma they never anticipated. Despite their dedication to inclusivity, they are confronted with the harsh reality that their current vaccine policy may not be sustainable in the face of a public health crisis.

Through sharp wit and insightful commentary, Jonathan Spector's play invites audiences to ponder the complexities of modern-day dilemmas and the difficult decisions that shape our communities.
