About the Show

Sam faces eviction, questioning the value of his possessions in a new comedy starring Danny DeVito.

Sam, a recluse surrounded by a multitude of possessions, faces a government order to clean up his property or risk eviction, leading him to confront the distinction between trash and treasure. This heartfelt new comedy stars Danny DeVito alongside his daughter, Lucy DeVito, and Ray Anthony Thomas. Written by Theresa Rebeck and directed by Moritz von Stuelpnagel, who previously collaborated on Bernhardt/Hamlet.
