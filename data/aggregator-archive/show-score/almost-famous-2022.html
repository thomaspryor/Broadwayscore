About the Show

A young man finds his place in the world through rock & roll in this musical based on the 2000 film.

In 1973 America, Rock and Roll dominates the airwaves, Richard Nixon is head of the nation, and starry-eyed 15-year-old William Miller dreams of being a music journalist.

When he is hired by Rolling Stone magazine to go on the road with a hot new band, William is thrust into the wild world of rock-and-roll, where his dreams, his needs, and his morals collide.
