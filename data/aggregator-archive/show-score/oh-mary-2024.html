About the Show

A Pulitzer Prize finalist, Cole Escola's Tony-winning comedy explores the forgotten dreams of Mary Todd Lincoln.

The play navigates themes of unfulfilled longing, alcoholism, and repressed aspirations, offering a unique perspective on Mrs. Lincoln's inner turmoil.

Through its one-act structure, the production unveils the layers of Mary's forgotten life and dreams, long overshadowed by her husband's legacy.

Oh, Mary! ultimately serves as a platform to reexamine and humanize the often-overlooked figure of Mary Todd Lincoln. By intertwining dark humor with profound introspection, the play offers a fresh perspective on her journey, shedding light on the complexities of her emotions and experiences amidst the backdrop of a pivotal moment in American history.

Oh, Mary! won 2 Tony AwardsÂ® in 2025, including Best Play, Best Performance by a Leading Actor in a Play for Cole Escola, and Best Direction of a Play for Sam Pinkleton.
