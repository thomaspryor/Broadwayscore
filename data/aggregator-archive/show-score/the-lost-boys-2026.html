About the Show

A cult favorite rises again on Broadway in a thrilling, blood-pumping new vampire musical.

Based on the 1987 cult film, The Lost Boys follows two teenage brothers who move with their family to a small California town — only to discover that fitting in comes with a supernatural twist. As they navigate friendship, family, and first love, they’re drawn into a world of mystery, rebellion, and local vampires lurking beneath the town’s sunny exterior

This new Broadway production is directed by Tony Award winner Michael Arden (Parade, Once on This Island, Maybe Happy Ending), bringing a cinematic classic to the stage with a mix of nostalgia and theatrical spectacle.

The Lost Boys joins Broadway’s growing lineup of musical adaptations that transform iconic films into live experiences, combining rock-infused energy with a story of youth, danger, and the eternal allure of the night.
