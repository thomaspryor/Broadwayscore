About the Show

Winner of the 2025 Pulitzer Prize for Drama, the Jasper family's drama unfolds in this Tony-winner for Best Play.

Purpose is a gripping family drama from the Tony Award-winning playwright of Appropriate Branden Jacobs-Jenkins and directed by two-time Tony Award winner Phylicia Rashad. The play centers on the influential Jasper family, long considered a cornerstone of Black American politics, with deep roots in civil rights, religion, and public service. However, like any family, they harbor hidden cracks and secrets that threaten their unity.

When the youngest son, Nazareth, unexpectedly returns home to Illinois with an uninvited guest, the Jasper family is thrust into a confrontation with their past. As they grapple with issues of faith, identity, and the legacies of Black radicalism, long-buried tensions come to light, challenging the familyâ€™s bonds.

Filled with humor, intrigue, and spirited dialogue, Purpose is an epic exploration of family, faith, and the complexities of heritage. This powerful play offers a fresh perspective on the enduring struggles and triumphs within a family deeply intertwined with the history of Black American politics.

Purpose won 2 Tony AwardsÂ® in 2025, including Best Play, and Best Performance by a Featured Actress in a Play for Kara Young.
