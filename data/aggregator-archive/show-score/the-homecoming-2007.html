About the Show

Olivier-nominee Matthew Dunster directs this adaptation of Harold Pinter‚Äôs dysfunctional family drama.

University professor, Teddy, brings his wife, Ruth, back to his childhood home to meet his domineering father, Max, and his antagonistic brothers, Lenny and Joey. The atmosphere quickly becomes tense as Ruth's presence disrupts the family dynamics. Unexpected secrets are unveiled, and Ruth's mysterious past adds to the intrigue. Amidst manipulation, desire, and psychological warfare, Ruth cunningly asserts her dominance, leaving the family members entangled in a twisted web of love, power, and betrayal...

Olivier Award-nominated director Matthew Dunster ('2:22 A Ghost Story') helms this new adaptation of Harold Pinter's drama, which stars BAFTA Award-winning Jared Harris ('Chernobyl'), Joe Cole ('Gangs of London'), and Lisa Diveney ('The Green Green Grass').
