About the Show

A revival of Ossie Davis' classic play, starring Leslie Odom Jr., directed by Kenny Leon.

Purlie, a traveling preacher, comes back to his Georgia hometown to rescue the local church and cotton pickers on Cap'n Cotchiepee's plantation. Ossie Davis wrote and starred in the original 1961 Broadway production alongside Ruby Dee. They reprised their roles in the 1963 film adaptation Gone Are the Days! A musical version premiered on Broadway in 1970, featuring Melba Moore's standout performance and the hit song "I Got Love."

Tony and Grammy winner Leslie Odom Jr. makes his return to the Broadway stage in the first-ever revival of Ossie Davis' classic. Odom, making his first Broadway bow since his Tony-winning performance in Hamilton, will star in the title role with Kenny Leon directing.
