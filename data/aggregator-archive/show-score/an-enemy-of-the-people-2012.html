About the Show

Jeremy Strong & Michael Imperioli lead a gripping revival about betrayal amid a small town's crisis.

Jeremy Strong, known for his role in Succession, and Michael Imperioli from The Sopranos and The White Lotus join forces in an enthralling revival of Henrik Ibsen's powerful classic, An Enemy of the People, returning to Broadway for a 16-week run.

In this compelling play, a conscientious small-town physician (Strong) prides himself on being an upright member of his tightly-knit community. However, when he discovers a catastrophe that risks the lives of everyone in town, he sounds the alarm. His world is shattered when those in authority, including his own brother (Imperioli), not only attempt to silence him but also plot his downfall.

Presented in a strictly limited engagement, this fresh adaptation comes from the creative mind of Tony Award¬Æ nominee Amy Herzog, renowned for her reimagining of Ibsen's A Doll's House last Broadway season, and is helmed by Tony Award-winning director Sam Gold.
