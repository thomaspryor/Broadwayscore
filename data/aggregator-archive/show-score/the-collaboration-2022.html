About the Show

Paul Bettany & Jeremy Pope star in a play about Andy Warhol & Jean-Michel Basquiat's famous exhibit.

In 1984 New York City, Andy Warholâ€™s light is fading. The wunderkind of the modern art scene, Jean-Michel Basquiat, agrees to collaborate with Warhol on a new exhibition, which soon becomes the talk of the city. Based on a true story, the two artists embark on an artistic and personal journey that redefines both of their worlds.

Direct from a hit run in London's West End, Paul Bettany ('WandaVision,' 'The Avengers') and Jeremy Pope ('Choir Boy,' 'Ain't Too Proud') reprise their roles for this Broadway production.
