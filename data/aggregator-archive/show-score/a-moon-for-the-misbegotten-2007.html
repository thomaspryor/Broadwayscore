About the Show

Michael Shannon and Ruth Wilson star in Rebecca Frecknall's revival of Eugene O'Neill's familial drama.

On a desolate Connecticut farm in 1923, Josie Hogan dreams of a life beyond the confines of her family's land. Abandoned by her brothers and tethered to her volatile father, her aspirations seem distant. James Tyrone Jr., their landlord, is a man haunted by his past, seeking solace in alcohol and fleeting connections. When Josie and James share a moonlit night, truths emerge, altering their lives irrevocably.

Eugene Oâ€™Neill's A Moon for the Misbegotten is a poignant exploration of redemption, illusion, and the human yearning for connection. Directed by Rebecca Frecknall (A Streetcar Named Desire, Cabaret), this production stars Michael Shannon (George & Tammy, Boardwalk Empire) and Ruth Wilson (The Affair, Luther).

The cast also includes Peter Corboy, Akie Kotabe, and David Threlfall.
