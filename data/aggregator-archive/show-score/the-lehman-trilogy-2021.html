About the Show

Production Covid-19 Safety Protocols

The Tony Award-winning play charting three generations of a family navigating American ambition and capitalism.

The three Lehman brothers have immigrated in search of the American Dream: from Bavaria to 1800s Alabama, where they open a store. Fastforward a couple of generations and the Lehman Brothers has collapsed, triggering the biggest financial crisis in history. This is the story of the three brothers, their children, and their grandchildren, and a critique of the changing nature of American capitalism.
