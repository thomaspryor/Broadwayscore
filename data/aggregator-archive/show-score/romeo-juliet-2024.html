About the Show

Modern retelling of Shakespeare's tragedy propels star-crossed lovers toward inevitable doom.

In a society defined by parental strife, Romeo and Juliet, consumed by impetuous ardor, hurtle towards their inevitable doom. What begins as an intoxicating whirlwind of passion swiftly descends into a maelstrom of brutal chaos, leaving only one path forward.

Kit Connor and Rachel Zegler, guided by the visionary direction of Sam Gold, breathe fresh life into Shakespeare's timeless tragedy, offering a visceral and resonant portrayal that speaks to a new generation on the precipice of love and loss.
