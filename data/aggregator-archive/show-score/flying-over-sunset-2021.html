About the Show

A brand new euphoric musical about a trio of celebrities on an LSD trip, directed by James Lapine.

Flying over Sunset is an entertaining fictional imagining of three celebrities -- Cary Grant, Clare Boothe Luce, and Aldous Huxley -- on a group LSD trip in Malibu, 1950. Together, they experience the colorful, reflective and psychedelic sides of the human condition: hope, love, and the search for meaning. The musical stars Carmen Cusack, Harry Hadden-Paton, and Tony Yazbeck.
