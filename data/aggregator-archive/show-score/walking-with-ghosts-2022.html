About the Show

Production Covid-19 Safety Protocols

Irish actor Gabriel Byrne's autobiographical solo show about his childhood and career.

Part memory play - looking back on an upbringing in a long changed Ireland, part examination of fame, Walking with Ghosts tells a full story that pays homage to the individuals and the places that shape our lives. 

In his autobiographical solo show, Gabriel Byrne (Hereditary) explores his identity as an immigrant driven from his home country, as well as the memories of love and regret that come with age.

The show will play a limited run of 75 performances only.
