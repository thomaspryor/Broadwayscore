About the Show

Jodie Comer stars as a talented lawyer, who must face a harrowing case that pushes her morals.

Tessa is at the top of her game- she's a young, smart lawyer who adores winning. She's worked her way up from her working class beginnings to the top of her field- defending, cross examining and illuminating the shadows of doubt in any case. But a surprising case will force her to face where the patriarchal power of the law, burden of proof and morals diverge.

Jodie Comer, the Emmy and Bafta Award-winning star of TV‚Äôs Killing Eve as well as Free Guy and The Last Duel, makes her Broadway debut in the American premiere of Suzie Miller‚Äôs award-winning play, which boldly explores how emotion and experience can clash with the rules of the game. Justin Martin directs this expert solo show.
