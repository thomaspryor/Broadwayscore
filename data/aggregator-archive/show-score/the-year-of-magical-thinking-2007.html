A one-woman adaptation of Joan Didion's autobiographical memoir.

The Year of Magical Thinking is the first New York remounting of the work by Joan Didion, directed by Jonathan Silverstein, and performed by Award-winning actress Kathleen Chalfant as a solo show. 

Adapted from her acclaimed memoir, The Year of Magical Thinking tells the story of Didionâ€™s journey of loss, determination, and ultimately hope, using her well-known wit to create an intimate snapshot of the strength of the human heart.

Performed in various locations throughout New York City.
