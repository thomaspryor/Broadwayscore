About the Show

Audra McDonald stars as Mama Rose in George C. Wolfe's Tony AwardÂ® nominated revival of GYPSY.

Set against the backdrop of the waning vaudeville era, GYPSY chronicles the relentless ambition of Mama Rose as she pushes her daughters, June and Louise, toward stardom. Rose's unwavering determination to secure fame for her children leads them through a series of performances, ultimately culminating in Louise's unexpected transformation into the legendary burlesque performer, Gypsy Rose Lee. The narrative delves into themes of maternal sacrifice, identity, and the pursuit of the American Dream.â€‹

This Broadway revival features six-time Tony AwardÂ® winner Audra McDonald in the role of Rose, marking the first time a Black actress has portrayed this iconic character on Broadway. McDonald's extensive credits include acclaimed performances in Carousel, Ragtime, and Lady Day at Emerson's Bar and Grill. Directed by five-time Tony AwardÂ® winner George C. Wolfe (Angels in America, Bring in 'da Noise, Bring in 'da Funk), the production also stars Tony AwardÂ® winner Danny Burstein (Moulin Rouge!, Fiddler on the Roof) as Herbie, Joy Woods (Six, Little Shop of Horrors) as Louise, and Jordan Tyson (The Notebook) as June.
