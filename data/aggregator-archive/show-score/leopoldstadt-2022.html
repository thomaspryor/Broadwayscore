About the Show

Tom Stoppard returns to Broadway with his newest heart-wrenching (and Olivier Award-winning) play.

Set in the Jewish quarter of Vienna, this impassioned story of heart and bravery follows an extended family from the late 1800s into the middle of the 20th century. Rife with the wit and charm that Tom Stoppard has come to be known for, his newest play explores fifty years of history across just two hours.

With a massive cast featuring 38 performers, and direction by Patrick Marber, the play is both grand in scale and laser-focused in its intimate look at human life.

The premiere production of the play received London's 2020 Olivier Award for Best New Play.
