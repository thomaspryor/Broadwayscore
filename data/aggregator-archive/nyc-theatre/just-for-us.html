Why see Just For Us?

us tour

After a rapturous response after a world premiere at Chicago's Cherry Lane Theatre and an extended Off-Broadway engagement, Alex Edelman's one-man show now takes off on tour.

Produced by Jenny Gersten, Rachel Sussman, and Seaview along with Mike Birbiglia, 'Just For Us' delves into the comedian's experiences with online trolling, specifically including anti-semitism. Instead of dismissing them, Edelman went one further and infiltrated his local White Nationalist group,  coming face to face with the keyboard warriors, culminating in an exploration of empathy, community and comedy.
